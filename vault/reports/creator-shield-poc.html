Concept

Creator Shield is a security service model for creators and public-facing small brands exposed to account takeover, impersonation, doxxing, phishing, stalker behavior, and reputation damage.

Threat model

  • Credential reuse and weak account recovery paths.
  • SIM-swap and email-compromise risk.
  • Impersonation profiles and fake sponsor outreach.
  • Public information that makes social engineering easier.

Service flow

The operating model combines intake, exposure review, account hardening, password manager onboarding, MFA review, recovery-plan creation, impersonation monitoring, and recurring check-ins.

Business value

The value proposition is direct: protect identity, access, audience trust, revenue, and personal safety before an incident turns into a public crisis.
